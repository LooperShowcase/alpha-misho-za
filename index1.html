Cars

The Nissan Skyline GT-R (Japanese: 日産・スカイラインGT-R , Hepburn: Nissan Sukairain GT-R) is a sports car based on the Nissan Skyline range. The first cars named "Skyline GT-R" were produced between 1969 and 1972 under the model code KPGC10,
and were successful in Japanese touring car racing events.
This model was followed by a brief production run of second-generation cars, under model code KPGC110, in 1973.
